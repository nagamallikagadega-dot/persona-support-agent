BILLING POLICY DOCUMENT

1. PAYMENT TERMS
All payments are due within 30 days of invoice date.
We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payments.
Late payments incur 2% monthly interest charge.

2. REFUND POLICY
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Eligible for refund within 30 days of purchase.
Duplicate charges are refunded immediately upon verification.
Contact billing@support.com for refund requests.

3. SUBSCRIPTION BILLING
Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same date each month.
Annual subscriptions receive 20% discount.
Cancellations take effect at end of billing period.

4. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Billing disputes must be raised within 60 days.
Our team investigates all disputes within 3 business days.
Escalated disputes are handled by senior billing team.

5. INVOICE MANAGEMENT
Invoices are sent via email automatically.
Download invoices from your account dashboard.
Contact accounts@support.com for invoice copies.
