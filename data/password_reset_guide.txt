PASSWORD RESET GUIDE

1. HOW TO RESET YOUR PASSWORD

Step 1: Go to login page
Step 2: Click Forgot Password link
Step 3: Enter your registered email address
Step 4: Check your email for reset link
Step 5: Click the reset link valid for 24 hours
Step 6: Enter your new password
Step 7: Confirm new password
Step 8: Click Reset Password button

2. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

3. COMMON ISSUES

Email not received:
- Check spam folder
- Wait 5 minutes and try again
- Contact support if still not received

Link expired:
- Reset links expire after 24 hours
- Request a new reset link

Account locked:
- Account locks after 5 failed attempts
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
- Contact support for immediate unlock
